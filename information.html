Lundi Studio is a communication design practice founded by Isobelle McCall and Kilian HÃ¶ppner. Our projects embody an interdisciplinary approach and a collective curiosity to learn. Research is an integral part of our design process, ensuring that our projects respond to their sociocultural contexts. At Lundi, we believe that playfulness is something to be nurtured within the creative process; that with experimentation that challenges convention, comes something beautiful.


Located in Melbourne and Berlin, Lundi recognises the importance of reflecting on the past and understanding the context of the present, acknowledging the complexities of history that inform where we stand today. Lundi acknowledges that we are on the lands of the Wurundjeri and Boon Wurrung peoples of the Kulin Nations, whose sovereignty was never ceded. We pay our respects to their Elders, past, present, and emerging, and extend that respect to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.
